HARRY POTTER

BY: J. K. Rowling

4.5

The novels follow Harry Potter, an 11-year-old boy who discovers he is the son of famous wizards and will attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

THE SILLENT PATIENT

BY: ALEX MICHAELIDES

4.4

The Silent Patient is a shocking psychological thriller of a woman's act of violence against her husband―and of the therapist obsessed with uncovering her motive. Alicia Berenson's life is seemingly perfect.

THE CHRONICLES OF NARNIA

BY: C. S. LEWIS
4.4

The main themes of The Chronicles of Narnia are love, sacrifice, and redemption. In the first novel, The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe, the Pevensie children enter Narnia for the first time.

One of Us Is Next

BY: Karen M. McManus
4.0

It follows three Bayview High students—Maeve Rojas, Knox Myers, and Phoebe Lawton—as an anonymous texter begins a game of truth or dare that has fatal consequences.

THE GREAT GATSBY

BY: F. Scott Fitzgerald
4.3

The novel tells the tragic story of Jay Gatsby, a self-made millionaire, and his pursuit of Daisy Buchanan, a wealthy young woman whom he loved in his youth.

THE KITE RUNNER

BY: KHALED HOSSEINI
4.3

The unforgettable, heartbreaking story of the unlikely friendship between a wealthy boy and the son of his father's servant, caught in the tragic sweep of history, The Kite Runner transports readers to Afghanistan at a tense and crucial moment of change and destruction.
